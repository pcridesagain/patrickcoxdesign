How did the Canopy UX Team Succeed in a Full Redesign?

Leading a complete application redesign

Sparked by a company realignment and rebrand, the Canopy UX embarked on a highly successful product redesign that journeyed through a company leadership transition, a designer hiring process, a Sketch to Figma migration, and a global pandemic.

The Problem

In the fall of 2019 Canopy pivoted. Up until then, the target market solution was an all-in-one Tax Resolution + Tax Preparation + Document Storage + Practice Management + Resource Management digital product.

After pivoting to focus entirely on Practice Management, the hastily bolted together suite was now a tangled mess of disjointed workflows that confused all the new customers. Oh, there was also a complete rebrand of the company!

The Solution

A five month overhaul and redesign of the entire core product suite including; information architecture, content design, user interface posture, reusable components, design system, overall look and feel, and a new brand.
